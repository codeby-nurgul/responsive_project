Get Involved: Connect with Green Groups

TEMA Foundation

Founded in 1992 by Hayrettin Karaca and A. Nihat Gökyiğit, TEMA focuses on combating soil erosion, afforestation, and protecting natural resources.
Their slogan is "Turkey Shall Not Become a Desert." They aim to raise awareness about soil erosion and environmental protection.

ÇEVKO Foundation

Established in 1991, ÇEVKO promotes recycling and waste management by collaborating with industries, local governments, and consumers.
Their goal is to reduce environmental pollution and promote efficient use of resources.

Turkish Nature Conservation Society

Founded in 1955, TTKD works to protect natural beauty and biodiversity.
The organization fights against environmental degradation and raises public awareness.

Nature Association

Established in 2002, this organization focuses on protecting biodiversity and advocating for nature's rights.
They implement conservation projects and educational programs to increase public awareness.

Greenpeace Mediterranean

Operating since 1995, Greenpeace Mediterranean advocates for renewable energy sources, opposes fossil fuels and nuclear energy, and works to establish marine reserves and sustainable fishing policies.

DenizTemiz Association

Founded in 1994, TURMEPA works to prevent marine pollution and protect marine ecosystems.
Their mission is to make the protection of Turkey's seas and coastal areas a national priority.
